QUIZZARDS

Feeling smart off late? You'd hate to miss this then! A crisp and fun assessment that can gauge your retention, activate your brain cells and check your technical knowledge.

Venue : Maharaja Agrasen Institute Of Technology

Date and Time : 31 March 11:30 AM

Rules

Only individual participation allowed. The contest will contain questions on technical aspects with some mind-twisters. Accuracy and time efficiency will decide your eligibility for the next round.

The event contains 3 rounds.

• MCQ Round

• Rapid Fire Round

• BUZZER Round

The details are as follows:.

MCQ Round

Each participant will get a set of 20-25 questions. Rank of the participant will change dynamically according to the accuracy and speed of answering each question..

Rapid Fire Round

In this round the participants will be given a set of 15-20 questions. These questions have to be solved in the specified time limit which will be made known to the participants beforehand.

10 points will be awarded for each correct answer.

Top participants will qualify for the next round that is, the buzzer round.

BUZZER Round

Questions will be displayed on the screen. Participant who presses the buzzer the earliest will get the chance to answer first. Every correct answer will fetch the participant 15 points. Whereas, the participant will lose 5 points for a wrong answer.

The participant with the maximum number of points at the end of the buzzer round will be the ultimate winner of QUIZZZARDS.

FAQ's
Q) Can one participate as a team?

No, only individuals can participate in this event.

Q) Is this a single-track event or will it have multiple events?

The event has three rounds, MCQ round, Rapid Fire Round and Buzzer Round.

Q) What is the scoring pattern?

The scoring pattern would be revealed to the participants at the time of the event
