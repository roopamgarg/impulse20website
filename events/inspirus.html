INSPIRUS

Hey, ever think about all the wonderful business ideas people around the world come up with? Want to create your own? We have just the thing for you! Propose an idea. Form strategies. Enhance them and change perceptions!

Venue : Maharaja Agrasen Institute Of Technology

Date and Time : 1 April 09:00 AM

Rules

This event will comprise of two rounds , details of each round are given below.

PRELIMINARY ROUND

In the prelim round a problem statement will be issued. Each team needs to come up with a solution. The solution should be submitted in PDF format only. Teams that come up with the most efficient solutions would be shortlisted. Only the shortlisted teams would further go through to the final round. Teams are limited to a maximum of 3 participants each. The PDF to be submitted must follow the following format.

The PDF should contain 3 pages.

Page 1 should have the TEAM’S NAME and the details of all the MEMBERS.

Page 2 should contain the solution to the problem

Page 3 should include the future scope and any other relevant details or information.

MAIN EVENT

Shortlisted teams from the prelim round will be invited to present their idea on the day of the event at a specified location that would be provided to the selected teams beforehand. On the day of the event, all shortlisted teams need to deliver a presentation, elaborating their ideas. The presentation should include different aspects and should also demonstrate how the proposed idea enhances the existing strategy. The use of graphical and statistical data is necessary.

• The evaluation method to assign a score to each team will be disclosed at the time of the main event.

• Final presentation should include the elaboration of the current situation along with the explanation as to how the proposed idea will improve the existing situation.

FAQ's
Q) How many members can one team have?

Teams can only have three members each.

Q) How to submit the solution to the problem given during the prelim round?

All the details regarding the submission of the solution for the prelim round would be provided to the teams that take part. Only PDF format would be accepted

Q) Is the use of graphs and stats necessary?

Yes, use of statistical and graphical data is necessary.
